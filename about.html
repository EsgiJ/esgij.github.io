About

Game Engine Developer / C++ Enthusiast

I am currently studying Computer Engineering at Yıldız Technical University, with a strong focus on coding and software development. I also had the opportunity to study at Czech Technical University for one semester through the Erasmus program, which allowed me to explore 10 different countries and broaden my cultural perspective.

My passion for creativity and technology extends beyond programming. During high school, I was actively involved in graffiti and illustration, which later evolved into an interest in 3D character art during my university years.

Additionally, under the guidance of artist Volkan Aslan, I participated in a project for the 16th Istanbul Biennial, titled "The Seventh Continent," at İKSV Alt Kat: Learning and Interaction Space. Along with a group of students, we created artworks related to the biennial's conceptual framework. Our works were later exhibited on the sailing vessel "Blue Panda," which toured various locations in Istanbul, in collaboration with the World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF).

Throughout high school, I attended several symposiums, further enriching my artistic and intellectual background. Currently, I am developing my own game engine called Luden Engine using C++, and I am working on a ray tracer, which combines my interests in graphics programming and game development.

In addition to my technical and artistic pursuits, I enjoy exploring new cuisines, reading books, and watching films, all of which inspire my creative endeavors.
